详细描述

This class defines a sphere.

It also computes coordinate transformations between cartesian and spherical coordinates. The spherical coordinates are valid in the ranges:

The coordinates are related as follows: is the angle in the xy plane starting with 0. from the positive x axis, is measured against the positive z axis. 

*
*        \      | Z
*         \theta|
*          \    |    .
*           \   |   .
*            \  |  .
*             \ | .
*              \|.
* --------------+---------.---------
*              /|\       .          Y
*             /phi\     .
*            /  |  \   .
*           /   |   \ .
*          /.........\
*         /     |
*      X /
